Powerful Features for Modern Property Management

One platform to manage every aspect of your vacation rental business — from reservations to reviews.

Centralized Property Dashboard

View all your properties, units, and their status in one clean dashboard. Never lose track of what's happening across your portfolio.

Smart Booking Calendar

Visual multi-property reservation calendar with drag-and-drop, availability management, and conflict detection across all platforms.

Automated Guest Communication

Pre-arrival, check-in, during-stay, post-stay, and review request messages sent automatically. Delight guests without lifting a finger.

Guest Profiles & History

Build rich guest profiles with stay history, preferences, notes, and communication logs to deliver personalized experiences every time.

Cleaning Task Management

Assign cleaning tasks, track completion status, attach checklists, and get notified the moment a property is ready for the next guest.

Maintenance Tracking

Log maintenance requests, assign to vendors, set priorities, and track resolution — keeping your properties in peak condition.

Payment Tracking

Track deposits, balances, payments received, and outstanding amounts. Know exactly where every dollar stands across all reservations.

Owner Reporting

Generate professional owner statements with occupancy, revenue, expenses, and property performance metrics — keep your owners informed and happy.

Staff Roles & Permissions

Control who sees and does what. Set granular permissions for managers, cleaners, maintenance staff, and owner-facing roles.

Digital Check-In

Guests complete check-in forms digitally before arrival. Collect IDs, signatures, agreements, and emergency contacts automatically.

Document Uploads

Store property documents, contracts, licenses, photos, and guest IDs securely — accessible from anywhere, at any time.

Mobile-Friendly Admin Panel

Manage your entire business from your phone. Responsive design works perfectly on any device, anywhere in the world.
